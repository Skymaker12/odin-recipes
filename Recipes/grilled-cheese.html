The Best Grilled Cheese Recipe

The best grilled cheese recipe is all about quantity. The right amount of cheese, sauce and toppings are all you need to create the most delicious grilled cheese you will ever make.

Ingredients

  1. Bread
  2. Butter
  3. Cheese

Instructions

  • Cut Two Pieces Of Bread
  • Butter One Side Of Both
  • Place One Buttered Bread (Butter Side Down) On Pan
  • Lightly Sprinkle Cheese Overtop
  • Place Second Slice Of Buttered Bread On top (Buttered Side Facing Up)
  • Turn Stove On Medium Heat
  • Flip After 5 Minuets
  • Take Grilled Cheese Off And Enjoy!
