Description

The Best Pizza Recipe

The best pizza recipe is all about quantity. The right amount of cheese, sauce and toppings are all you need to create the most delicious pizza you will ever make.

Ingredients

  1. Dough
  2. Sauce
  3. Cheese
  4. Any Extra Topping

Instructions

  • Form Dough Into Flat Circle
  • Pinch Outside To Create Crust
  • Spread Sauce Evenly Across Dough
  • Cover Dough In Cheese (Only a few handfuls)
  • Place Pizza In Oven For 10 Minuets
  • Take Out Of Oven And Cut Accordingly
